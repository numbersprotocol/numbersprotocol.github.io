Data Is Life

Full Privacy

Numbers brings together decentralized technology, data framework, and an ecosystem for data and related services. These technologies serve users and provide services with FULL PRIVACY, SECURITY, AND CONSENT.

Life Analysis

User can use Numbers App to record interesting data in their LIFE and save it as ASSETS. From the data VISUALIZATION, users can easily understand their personal data and discover interesting analysis.

Data Network

Datapedia is a place which can be accessed from Numbers App. In Datapedia, users can gain and share DATA-BASED KNOWLEDGE and create a data network powered by users.
